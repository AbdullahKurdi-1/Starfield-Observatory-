How It Works

Five steps.
Zero effort on your part.

01
We Pick Up Your Telescope

We drive to your door, collect your equipment in padded protective cases, and transport it safely to our Al-Ula dark-sky site. Fully insured.

We Come to You

We Image All Night

Your telescope captures your chosen target — a galaxy, nebula, or star cluster — through the entire night under Bortle Class 1 skies while you sleep.
